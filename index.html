The Ollie

The ollie is a skateboarding trick where the rider and board leap into the air without the use of the rider's hands. In 1976, Alan Gelfand, whose nickname is "Ollie", learned to perform no-handed aerials in bowls and pools using a gentle raising of the nose and scooping motion to keep the board with the feet.
